Langara English for Academic Purposes (LEAP)

LEAP is an intensive English program designed to prepare students whose first language is not English for full-time study at English language universities and colleges.

  • Nine levels, from beginner to advanced
  • Seven-week sessions
  • 20 hours of classroom instruction per week
  • Classes scheduled Monday to Friday, 8:30am – 4:30pm
  • Approximately two to four hours of homework per day
  • Integrated skills (develop all four skills together)
  • Academic topics
  • Graduation from LEAP 8 leads to direct admission to university studies at Langara

Details please view: Langara English for Academic Purposes

Langara English Proficiency Program (LEPP)

LEPP’s part-time courses are for improving language skills in the evenings and on Saturday morning to reach the goal of having qualified instruction with an interactive, focused learning environment.

LEPP offers two programs for students: Essential Skills and Expanded Skills.

In Essential Skills courses, students work on developing their listening, speaking, writing, and pronunciation skills.

In Expanded Skills courses, students can focus on language development within areas of interest such as business communication, academic writing, and public speaking.

Explore Program

In June and July, this program provides English instruction, educational field trips, elective courses, and recreational activities for French Canadian and international students to have the opportunity to know other parts of Canada. Students live with host families in the Vancouver area during the program.

Details please view: Explore Program

Summer English Language Program (SELP)

Students take three or four-week program and live with a Canadian family to immerse in Canadian culture and natural environment in Vancouver, BC.
